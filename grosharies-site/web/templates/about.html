About

These days, everyone is writing their groceries list on a piece of paper hanging on the fridge, or just simply trying to remember the whole list (And eventually forgetting some of them).

We think that this is an old-fashioned way to manage the groceries list, so we offer you to try our brand new, cross-platform, highly comfortable web application!

This is exactly where 'Grosharies' come into place!

'Grosharies' allows multiple users to share and manage their groceries list via web-application that is accessible from any PC, Mac or mobile device. The list is synchronized between all of the users, in order for you to stay updated with it in any time and any place!
