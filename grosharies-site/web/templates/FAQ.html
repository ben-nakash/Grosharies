FAQ

  • How can I log in?
    You can choose to log in with your Google/Facebook account, or by supplying a Username and Password if already registered.

  • Can I add new people to a group after I already created it?
    Of course! you can add as many people as you want even after creating the group.

  • If I'm a member in a group and I created a new list, can I decide who can use the list?
    Yes, if you created the list, you become the list admin. The list admin can decide who is viewer and who is partner on your list.

  • If I'm a member in a group which has another person as a manager, can I still add new people to it?
    Yes. But they won't be added until the group's admin confirm it.

  • Is it possible to access your system via mobile phones?
    Yes, but at the moment we don't have a mobile application, so you can access it through your browsers like Chrome/Firefox/Safari.

  • Can i leave a group even if i'm the manager?
    Yes, it is possible, but if you want to keep the group you will have to choose a new manager instead. Otherwise the group will be deleted permanently.
